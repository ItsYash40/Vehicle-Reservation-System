Cookie Policy

Learn about how we use cookies to enhance your experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a better user experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enable essential website functionality.
  • Understand how you interact with our website.
  • Enhance your user experience by remembering your preferences.
  • Provide personalized content and targeted advertising.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us analyze how visitors use our website.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and enhance functionality.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver personalized ads.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may impact the functionality of our website.

  • To learn how to manage cookies in your browser, visit the browser's help section.
  • You can also opt out of personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party cookies provided by trusted partners for analytics and advertising purposes. These cookies are subject to the respective third party's privacy policies.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this page periodically to stay informed.
