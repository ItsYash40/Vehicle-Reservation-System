Cancellation Policy

Overview

At our Vehicle Reservation System, we understand that plans can change. This policy outlines the terms and conditions under which cancellations and refunds are handled.

Cancellation Charges

  • Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled time are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled time will incur a 50% cancellation fee.
  • No refunds will be provided for cancellations made less than 1 hour before the scheduled time.

How to Cancel

To cancel your reservation, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account on our website or app.
  2. Go to the "My Bookings" section.
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel and click "Cancel".
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the cancellation.

Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method. If you do not receive your refund within this time, please contact our support team.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our cancellation policy, feel free to contact our support team at support@vehiclereservation.com.
