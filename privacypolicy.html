Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your bookings without penalty up to 24 hours before the pickup time. After that, cancellation fees may apply.

  • Full refunds for cancellations made 24 hours in advance.
  • Partial refunds for late cancellations.
  • No refunds for no-shows.

Account Deletion

We respect your right to delete your account and personal data. To request account deletion:

  • Log in to your account and navigate to "Settings."
  • Click on "Delete Account" and follow the instructions.
  • Alternatively, contact our support team for assistance.

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You can:

  • Request to view or download your data.
  • Request corrections or updates to your data.
  • Request the permanent deletion of your data.

Third-Party Sharing

We may share your data with trusted third-party providers to enhance your rental experience, but only with your consent.

  • Marketing and analytics providers.
  • Payment processing services.
  • Partnered car rental agencies.
